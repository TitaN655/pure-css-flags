Scalable Pure-CSS Flags

A collection of flags rendered solely through CSS on HTML <div> tags. Using fussyflags by Michael Lascarides as a reference sorted losely by difficulty, Wikipedia's list of aspect ratios of national flags for accurate aspect ratios. The website schemecolor.com was also used in conjunction widt Wikipedia to gather the hex colours and cross-reference any of the more ambiguious colours (for example, Greece does not currently officially define a specific shade of blue/cyan for their flag). Of course, there is been plenty of my own flag construction research and cross-referencing to help ensure accurate measurements.
